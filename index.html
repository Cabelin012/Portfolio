CLINIC APPOINTMENT SYSTEM

Made with:

The Clinic Appointment System is designed to efficiently manage patient records and simplify the scheduling of medical appointments. The system supports two distinct user roles: Doctor and Staff. Doctors can view only their own appointment lists, helping them stay organized with upcoming consultations. Staff members have broader access—they can view all appointments and create new ones on behalf of patients. The system also automatically sends appointment notifications to patients to keep them informed and reduce no-shows. For added convenience, printable appointment summaries are generated, allowing both staff and patients to have a physical copy of scheduled visits.
