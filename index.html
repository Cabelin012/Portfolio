ANRDOID BASED MOTORCYCLING MONITORING SYSTEM

Made with:

MotoPotato is an Android-based monitoring system designed to enhance motorcycle safety by tracking real-time rider behavior, including speed, acceleration, and navigation patterns, using GPS and accelerometer data. The mobile app provides immediate feedback and alerts, promoting safer riding habits. The system significantly reduces unsafe practices and fosters a safety culture among riders. The research demonstrates that MotoPotato can be integrated into broader road safety policies, contributing to overall traffic safety efforts.

This application was developed in collaboration with my classmate Jerome Maraon.
